💡 Management Philosophy Tại sao cấu trúc này hoạt động

🎯 Tại sao cấu trúc này?

Mỗi workstream được assign cho người có expertise phù hợp nhất, không phải theo hierarchy. Kết quả: accountability rõ ràng, không có overlap, không có gap.

  • Sơn — Investment background → Acquisition lead
  • Dung — CFO → Finance & Cashflow owner
  • Phán — Project management → Construction owner
  • Hường — Procurement expertise → Cost control owner
  • Cả — Legal background → DD & compliance owner
  • Zera — AI orchestrator → Cross-workstream coordination
  • ExCom (Colin · Dung · Bach) → Project oversight & escalation

📈 Từ Forecast → Management System

Files bắt đầu là forecast/planning documents. Khi project move forward, chúng evolve thành live management tools — không bao giờ bị xoá, chỉ được evolve.

  • Phase 1: Feasibility Study → Financial Model (forecast)
  • Phase 2: Financial Model → Actual vs Forecast (live)
  • Phase 3: Dashboard → Management reporting (ongoing)
  • Principle: Baseline readonly, live data overlaid on top
  • MEMORY.md: Mỗi folder có MEMORY.md riêng, Zera tổng hợp
  • Versioning: v1 → v2 → v3... không bao giờ overwrite baseline
  • Archive: Project close → full archive, lessons learned

📏 Working Rules & Conventions How the team works together
📄

Versioning Rules

1
Naming convention: YYYYMMDD_FileName_vX.ext — date prefix + version suffix
2
No delete: Never delete files — archive to /archive/ subfolder instead
3
Change log: Every major update logged in MEMORY.md with date + what changed
4
Version numbering: v1 = first draft, v2 = major revision, vFinal = locked output
5
Output freeze: Once marked "Output", file is readonly — create new version to update
🤖

Zera Orchestrator Rules

1
Weekly summary: Every Monday 08:00 — Zera posts workstream status in Main Group
2
MEMORY update: After any major decision → Zera updates folder MEMORY.md + master
3
Cross-workstream flags: Zera alerts when action in one stream affects another
4
Sub-personality: Zera adapts tone per workstream (legal = formal, construction = direct)
5
Escalation: Blocked items >48h → auto-escalate to ExCom in Main Group
📝

MEMORY.md Convention

1
Per-folder: Each of 6 folders has its own MEMORY.md — owned by workstream lead
2
What to write: Decisions made, assumptions changed, risks identified, lessons learned
3
When to update: After every meeting, decision, or file version change
4
Master aggregation: Zera combines all 6 MEMORY.md → 00_Master/MEMORY.md weekly
5
No transcripts: Never paste full meeting transcripts — summarize key decisions only
📊

Forecast → Management System

1
Readonly baseline: Original forecast files are locked — never overwritten
2
Live update: Actuals overlaid in new columns/tabs — baseline always visible
3
Variance alerts: >10% variance triggers automatic flag to workstream lead
4
Quarterly review: ExCom reviews full forecast vs actual every quarter with Zera summary
5
Project close archive: All files archived with final actuals, accessible for 5 years
