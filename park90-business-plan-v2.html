Strategic Rationale
📈

Market Timing

HCMC HNWI count +12% YoY (Knight Frank 2025). Vietnam wine import +18% CAGR 2021–2025. The window is open — early mover advantage in premium urban wine lounge segment.

🍷

Positioning Advantage

Not a "hotel bar" — an "urban wine lounge" with separate branding and entrance. Targets HNWI + corporate entertainment (18,000 potential users), not tourist or casual walk-in.

💰

Park90 COGS Edge

Park90 distributor advantage: –40% COGS via exclusive distribution + prepay discount. Results in 28% COGS vs 35% industry standard. 7-point margin edge = $5,700/month at base case.

⚖️

Legal Structure Separation

Operating entity ≠ Park90 distribution entity. Wine bar buys at market –20% (not –40%). Park90's upside is equity stake + brand showroom for their portfolio. No channel conflict.

🎟️

Founding Members De-risk

Phase 0 invitation-only model: 200 founding members × $500 = $100K pre-revenue. Validates demand before committing Phase 1 capital. Also builds loyal core customer base from day 1.

🔒

Downside Protection

3 structured exit ramps cap maximum loss at $150K on a $300K investment. Phased structure means only $50K at risk until CP5 is validated. Risk-adjusted return profile is favorable.
