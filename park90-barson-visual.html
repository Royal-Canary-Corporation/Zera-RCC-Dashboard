Why This Model Works
Two constraints cancel each other out.
Barson's constraint: underutilized premium lobby space with no F&B identity.
Park90's constraint: D1 commercial rents price out premium wine bar economics.

Together: Barson activates the space at zero risk. Park90 accesses D1 without the rent premium that kills the unit economics. The distributor margin advantage (+40% vs market COGS) creates a structural profit engine that neither party could build alone.
